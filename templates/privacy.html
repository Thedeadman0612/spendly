Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and powering filters. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app on your behalf.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You can delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all associated data from our systems.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use trusted infrastructure providers (hosting, databases) to operate the service; these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on our website. We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 5 business days.
